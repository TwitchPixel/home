Jeff & Nat

Jeff Saamanen is a seasoned Animator and Artist with a wealth of experience spanning over two decades. He has been a cornerstone in the industry, having taught the Adobe Suite and 2D Animation for years, and contributing his talents to projects such as Dan Harmon's HarmonQuest through his work with Bardel Entertainment. Jeff's creative prowess extends beyond teaching, as he has served as a Designer, creative, and Director for numerous animation, film, and commercial endeavors.

Notably, his creative content has significantly bolstered the sales of small brands, amounting to hundreds of millions. Transitioning to entrepreneurship, Jeff co-founded Twitch Pixel Studios, where he played a pivotal role in creating original intellectual properties alongside Natalie for over two decades. Together, they pioneered innovative ventures, including Natalie Harvey Creative, where Jeff's expertise was instrumental in developing prototypes, production pipelines, and fulfillment strategies for groundbreaking products like the Easel.y and the Tin.y Canvas.

Furthermore, Jeff's diverse skill set shines through in his contributions to various projects, including the Live-action web series "4Villains" and the design, development, and production of "The Pirates of Haven" and "I'll See You In Valhalla" card and board games.

Natalie Harvey emerges as a natural-born, self-taught artist with a profound understanding of the art industry cultivated over a decade. Her expertise lies in the art supply chain, empowering fellow artists with the right tools and knowledge to excel in their craft.

Whether it's traditional mediums like canvas and clay or digital realms encompassing animation, character design, and graphic arts, Natalie's proficiency knows no bounds. Particularly in the digital sphere, she showcases her talents through videography, leveraging platforms like TikTok to influence and promote her brand and products available on NatalieHarveyCreative.com.

Alongside Jeff, Natalie serves as a skilled video editor, specializing in film, animation, podcasts, and advertising, consistently delivering artistic solutions to clients.

Together, Jeff Saamanen and Natalie Harvey form an unstoppable duo capable of tackling projects of grand proportions. With Jeff's expertise in brand building and project management, every endeavor they embark upon is meticulously planned from the ground up, from brand identity to production schedules, ensuring seamless execution.

Natalie's artistic flair and commitment to empowering fellow creators complement Jeff's strategic vision, resulting in a dynamic partnership that thrives on innovation and creativity. Their collaborative efforts have not only birthed successful ventures but have also left an indelible mark on the realms of animation, design, and entrepreneurship.
