Enter Email

1.

Click ‘Your email’ field and enter the email address you used to sign up with our community

Enter Member Name

2.

Click ‘Member Name’ field and enter the user name you use on members.twitchpixel.com

Click NEXT

3.

Click ‘Next’ (do not click "Continue" or you may loose the information you have input.)

The LORE Event

4.

Here you are presented with the LORE Event. Like a character sheet fill out this section to define the characteristics of the character as you envision them based on the image provided of them. Each section should help you define a clear picture of who this character is before moving on to the next step their story!

Name Your Character

5.

Click ‘Citizen Name’ And give your Citizen a Fun, Clever, Punny, or just plain ole' Name.

Select a District

6.

Select an option from the listed Districts throughout the world of Unicornitopia! To read more on each one to get an idea of the location and what your envisioned characters life might be like, follow the links to each locations LORE Item in our Encyclopedia Imajika.

Select Alignment / Disposition

7.

Select an Alignment or Disposition that best reflects your envisioned character.

Rate your Characters Defining Traits

8.

This List of Defining traits is to set the tone of your character, how they are as an individual in respect to different personality traits. For each item select how you feel your evisioned character would embody each one.

Select Next

9.

Click ‘Next’

Citizen Story: Past

10.

Now is your time to shine! With your LORE Event character defined you now have 1000 words to write up a history of your character, past events that changed their lives and shaped their personality. Key moments that would bring them to where they are now and define their whole life.

Citizen Story: Present

11.

Next you now have 1000 words to write up the current events of their life, where they are, what they are doing and what the prospects of their future might be. Try to think of terms of " Yes And" where what your create cn lend to the future stories fo this character if you'd like to see them grown and evolve over time!

Click Next

12.

Click ‘Next’

Agree to Community Terms & Rules of Conduct

13.

Click the ‘Yes I Confirm’ check box to agree to Community Terms and Conditions and our Rules of Conduct, ensuring your submission meets all rules and guidelines to be considered.

Agree to LORE Event Terms & Conditions

14.

Click ‘I Agree’ to agree to the LORE Event terms and conditions.

Submit!

15.

Click ‘Submit’ when you have finished to complete your submission, you can also enable the toggle to send yourself a copy of your submission.

Wait for Comfirmation

16.

You have successfully submitted your submission!

Complete the Event.

17.

Click ‘Continue’ Scroll Down and Select Continue to Complete the Event.

Select Lore Event to Check your Progress.

18.

Click the Main Lore Event title to your progression.

LORE Event Tracker

19.

Here you can view your current LORE Event progress. Check back to see of new events have launched to participate in more!

Completing lore events will have added benefits in the future so be sure to complete them!

View and Share Submission

20.

Click ‘Community’ to view the Community Channels.

21.

Find the LORE Events "SUBMISSIONS" channel and select it.

View Submissions

22.

Here you can view all the submissions and vote on the ones you like including your own! To find your submissions Look for your Member Name under Submitted By.

VOTE!

23.

Click the ‘👍, +1, thumbsup’ button To vote for any submission including your own select or click the default Thumbs-Up emoji to cast your vote! The Submissions with the most votes will move on to the next round!

Get Permalink to Share

24.

Click the three dots '...' in the upper right corner and select ‘Permalink’ to copy a direct link to your submission!

SHARE!

25.

Once you have Copies the Permalink you can share this link all around the web and social media to gets new users, friends, and family to come vote on your submission! Share on Twitter, Facebook, Instagram, wherever you interact with your friends family and community at large!
