Battle, Feast, Reapeat!
Will you be Victorious!?

The game plays out over 5 seasons, each consiting of two phases, a battle and a feast. Each phase has its own deck, the Orrusta(Battle) deck, and the Veisla (Feast) deck.

Battle Phase

Fall In.

As battle starts, each warrior must select one at a time a battle line to place their Viking in on the battlefield. Each battle line offers unique bonuses provided the Viking's Lineage matches.

Enter the Fray.

Step into the chaos and select your target rival to face in a one-on-one battle among the fray. Your skirmish can be influenced by the other warriors around you, so strategize and be vigilant!

Settle the Score

After both warriors in the skirmish tally their totals, you and the other Vikings may affect your or your rivals' success or failure with insults and war cries. After the dust settles, the true victor is declared!

Feast Phase

Order Up

Take a seat at the table, play food and drink to yourselves and one another all in an attempt to stay conscious throughout the celebration! Keep your fullness high and your inebriation low to be the last Viking Standing!

Skol!

Viking for cheers, Skol! Spike your opponent's drinks, water down your own with mixers, spice your opponents' food, or improve your own with garnishes as you all try to balance your food and drink to be the last Viking conscious!

Second Wind

With a song in your heart, improve your chances of success, but watch out for your rivals as they throw insults at you to try and demoralize you and win the day, while you fire off your own insults in retaliation!
