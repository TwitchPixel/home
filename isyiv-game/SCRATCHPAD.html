Choose Your Viking Warrior!

Select Your warriror and prepare for battle! Your warriror will compete for Odin's favor through Battles and feasts alike!

Step into the Fray, honor your lineage and face your rivals in the fray, earning you favor in feasts and battle! Fling insults, sing songs, and shout your Warcry across the battlefieled as you earn favor, as you all perpare for Ragnarok!

Battle for Odin's Favor

True to myth & legend, the everlasting battlefields of Valhalla await! Froge your story through the chaos, giving no quarter as you vie for Odin's Favor

Take your battlelines, stand your ground, and face your rivals in battle using your mastery over your weapons and with the favor of the gods at your side. Amidst the fray it is last man standing, show your rivals who Odin's true warriror is!

Feast & Revelry

After the chaos has ended and the battles decided, face your next challeng in the Feast Halls of Valhalla! Sling Drinks and Insults, Sing songs of Valor, and dine in the sacred halls of Valhalla to earn Odins Favor through your Revelry!

Spice and Garnish you and your rivals food, or add mixers and spikes to eachothers drinks, as you compete to be the last Viking standing at the end of the eve!
