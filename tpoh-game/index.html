Sea & Skies Await!

Gather your captain, ship, and crew: it's time to stake your claim on the mysterious and fractured world of Haven. The world may be shattered and drifting apart, but there's gold to be made for a pirate with an airship! Raid ports, take on bounties, smuggle cargo, or face dangerous creatures...


Then make your fellow captains' lives as miserable and dangerous as yours!As you find buried treasure, slay monsters, and commit other acts of derring-do, your tale will grow until it's told in ports, bars, and coves for centuries to come.

Outfit Your Ship

As commander of your very own airship, you'll need to seek out the best captain and crew to survive. As your story grows,you'll gain access to more powerful captains, crew members, and artillery pieces.

Take on the World

Sailing from port to port, you'll live every part of a pirate's life: bounty hunting, smuggling, treasure, navigation, even trade and politics.

Bet it all in Battle!

Take on your opponents in risk-meets-poker ship battles. Ante up, raise the stakes, and take the pot if you win. But be warned — the winning captain decides the consequences.

Backstab your Opponents

You're all in a race to be the most famed Sky Pirate of all, and sabotage is encouraged. Trip up your opponents with surprise Encounters — but expect them to get you back for it.

Become a Legend...No, a legacy

As you make your mark on Haven through combat,exploration, and piracy, your tale will grow from a mere Whisper to a Legacy — and with each rank, you'll face more powerful threats and fill your coffers with ever-rarer artifacts.

Choose your faction and
Write Your Story

Haven's a dangerous world: mysterious and ancient machines, a planet falling to pieces,and a group of factions fighting over the ruins. Are you looking to get filthy rich? Keep the peace? Commune with the gods? Who will you choose in your quest to leave your legacy?
